About Us

Our mission is to go beyond surface-level engagement

At JanNova, we help organisations evolve by unlocking the true potential of their people and co-create solutions for long-term results. We combine behavioural science and business insight to solve complex organisational challenges.

JanNova is built on a simple belief: organisations grow when their people do. Whether shaping culture or navigating change—we align people, culture, and performance for sustainable success.

  • Empower teams through experiential, evidence-informed learning.
  • Break silos, improve communication, nurture relationships.
  • Practical interventions that work in real organisational settings—not theory-only decks.

Our values

Kindness, openness, reliability, and enthusiasm—what we expect of ourselves and each other.

Our promises

WOW experiences, doing what we say, and making learning meaningful and measurable.

Our philosophy

  • People drive performance — No strategy succeeds without engaged, capable individuals.
  • Culture is a business driver — How people think, behave, and interact drives outcomes.
  • Change must be intentional — Structure, clarity, and commitment.
  • Solutions should be practical — What works in the real world, not just on paper.

People + New Beginnings = JanNova

Together, JanNova represents a fresh approach—not one-size-fits-all consulting, but interventions tuned to your reality.

Why choose JanNova?

  • We build around your people, culture, and reality.
  • Built on science, not guesswork—data and what actually works.
  • We work with you—no long decks and ghosting.
